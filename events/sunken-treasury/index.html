Sunken Treasury

Event • Underdocks
A path leads you into a partially submerged vault.
There are 2 chests but only 1 brittle key.

The First Chest: Shaking it, you get jingly-jangly noises. Bit of gold.
The Second Chest: Enormous, ornate, and clearly cursed. Lots of gold!
