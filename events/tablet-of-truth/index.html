Tablet of Truth

Event • Act 1 - Overgrowth
You defeat a pair of Guardian Kin and make your way into a protected vault.

Inside is a tablet with familiar inscriptions. Your intuition tells you that this language can be deciphered rather easily... However, once you begin deciphering you'll be locked in.

The tablet gives off a soothing energy and you can smash it to receive its healing energies instead.
